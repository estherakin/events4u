About us

We are two young girls who recognised the stress associated with event planning and hope to ease this. As people with experience in this field ourselves, we understand what others go through when planning their events. Consequently, we decided to make this website to connect event planners to their desires. Hope you enjoy our website!

Taking away the stress from event planning

This website will hopefully connect you to all the elements you need to make your event successful. It will show you all of the places around you that provide the service that you want. With the use of an interactive map, our site ensures that you know where the service is and how far it is from you. This will hopefully make your event planning easier and less stressful
